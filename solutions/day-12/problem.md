# Day 12 — Add Two Promises

**LeetCode Link:** https://leetcode.com/problems/add-two-promises/

**Difficulty:** Easy

**Topic:** Promises / Async

---

## Problem Statement

> Given two promises `promise1` and `promise2`, return a new promise that resolves with the sum of the two numbers.

## Examples

```
Input:  promise1 resolves with 2, promise2 resolves with 5
Output: 7

Input:  promise1 resolves with 10, promise2 resolves with -12
Output: -2
```

## Constraints

- promises resolve with numbers
